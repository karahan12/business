ABOUT US

“Les Cartes de Maman” is an independent card shop that launched March of 2020. We sell beautiful, curated pre-made cards that can be used for any event. We also have the option of custom-creation, where we create cards or invitations specialized to each client’s needs. Our designers work tirelessly creating new content and we have pre-made cards updated every two weeks and every custom creation is delivered within two weeks. “Les Cartes de Maman” focuses on details, artistry, and love.

THE TEAM

"Les Cartes de Maman" wouldn't be possible without the hard-working team.

AUREA KARA HAN, FOUNDER

Aurea has been in making cards with her mother ever since she was in middle school. She loves the creative freedom of making cards but having the structure and organization of the products. Aurea founded this company with love.


RACHEL QUON, CREATIVE DIRECTOR

Rachel has been in the creative industry for a while. She manages all the artistic finishes of the cards and ensures that they evoke the message “Les Cartes de Maman” aspires to spread.


ANDREA LAU, FINANCE DIRECTOR

Andrea has a background in finance, but always had an artistic outlet. Given the opportunity to join a card company that held near and dear to her was a dream come true. She makes sure all business components of the company are well taken care of.
